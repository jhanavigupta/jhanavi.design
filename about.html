Who am I?

  • 2001-2019
    I was born on March 20, 2001 in Patiala, Punjab. As a child I had a curiour nature and I loved to color. I completed my 10th in 2017 and 12th in 2019
  • 2019-2020
    When I began my Bachelors at Delhi University, I was eager to explore new opportunities and try my hand at different things. I delved into web development, but when Covid-19 hit India, I realized that coding was not my forte. Fortunately, this led me to the world of UI/UX design.
  • 2020-2021
    I taught myself graphic design and joined a university-based club to gain more experience. I also joined the technical team of The Placement Cell, which helped me to hone my skills even further. I was thrilled to secure my first internship.
  • 2021-2022
    Becoming the Technical Head of The Placement Cell was an incredibly exciting opportunity for me. It allowed me to apply my design skills in a leadership role and working closely with a team setup. At the same time, I continued to pursue internships and gain valuable experience in the industry.
  • 2022-2023
    After a journey of self-discovery, exploring different fields and gaining experience in the industry, I finally graduated with a clear vision of my career path in UX/UI design. My passion for design only grew stronger, and I was eager to pursue further education to enhance my skills and knowledge. That's when I decided to join the Masters in UX/UI Design program at Chitkara University.

Why UX/UI Design?

I am fascinated by the field of UX design. The process of understanding users and identifying their needs and pain points, and then crafting a solution that benefits both the user and the business, is a challenge that I am eager to tackle. I believe that good and simple design can make a significant impact on people's lives. Similarly, I am also drawn to the visual aspect of design, which is where UI comes in. I enjoy the process aesthetically presenting the design, making it appealing and intuitive for users to interact with.I love the fact that UX/UI design has a constantly evolving nature, is dynamic and has a holistic approach.
